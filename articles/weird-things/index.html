Weird Things

Written by Hedvika Olmer, 7 years
Published on January 26, 2021

There could be a lot of weird things just lying around your house. Take a closer look at all of them. You can add some weird things to this article when you finish reading it.

Weird Thing #1

These are two carrots that grew together. They twisted around each other and grew together at the top. You can take the two twisted parts apart but you can never take it apart into two twisty carrots because, as I already said, they grew together at the top.

Weird Thing #2

My dad discovered this when he was drinking his water with ice. He put his straw on the top of the ice cube and sucked on it really hard. It made a hole in the ice cube. I also tried but I didn’t make such a big hole. I did make a smaller hole. It is weird that by putting a straw on a block of ice, you can make a hole, isn’t it?

Weird Thing #3

While I was baking, I cut a piece of butter and there was a half circle that stayed on my knife. It made such a cool design that is very weird.

Weird Thing #4

When you unwrap a new piece of butter, part of the thing that was wrapped around the butter is imprinted on the butter.
